360° Panorama Viewer with Eye Frame

About

This is an interactive 360° panorama viewer with a binocular-style eye frame overlay. You can:

  • Click and drag to look around
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate
  • Click the VR button (bottom right) for VR mode
  • Works on mobile devices with gyroscope support

Embed This Panorama

To embed this panorama viewer in your website, use the following code:

<iframe width="1200" height="700" src="panorama.html" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen allow="accelerometer; gyroscope; vr" title="360 Panorama Viewer"> </iframe>
